About the Union

The NIN ST Employees Welfare Union is a registered employees’ welfare body representing Scheduled Tribe employees of ICMR–National Institute of Nutrition, Hyderabad. The Union works for the welfare, rights, and professional development of its members in accordance with Government of Telangana rules and regulations.

  • To represent genuine grievances before the competent authorities.
